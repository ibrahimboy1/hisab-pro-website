Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 26, 2025

Hisab Pro ('us', 'we', or 'our') values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register, we collect your name and email address (via Firebase Authentication). This is necessary for your identity and account security.
  • Financial Data: Any customer and transaction data you enter is saved on your local device (Room Database) and your personal Firebase account. This data is end-to-end encrypted, and we, the developers, cannot access it at all.
  • App Permissions: The app requests certain permissions: RECORD_AUDIO for 'Smart Voice Input' and WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to save reports on your phone.
