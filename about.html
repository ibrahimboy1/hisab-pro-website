Our Story

The Search for a Better Solution

Hisab Pro was created with the idea that accounting should be simple, not complicated. We saw small business owners and individuals still relying on pen and paper, which is prone to errors and time-consuming.

Technology That Helps

This isn't just an app; it's a modern solution. We used Android Architecture Components for a fast and reliable experience. Room Database provides an excellent offline mode, and Firebase keeps your data safe and synced.

About the Creator

This application was built by IT Boy Creator, whose passion is to solve everyday problems through technology. If you have any ideas or feedback, please contact us.
